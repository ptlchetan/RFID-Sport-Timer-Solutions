A typical RFID system consists of a reader, and antenna and a transponder.

RFID Systems use specially-designed lightweight tags that can be read up to 200 mph, need no charging, are disposable and cost just a few dollars per competitor. Perfect for any scoring and timing application you may have.

The ULTRA is the latest technology in the RFID world and is able to read both disposable and re-usable tags, making it extremely versatile catering for smaller club events through to the largest fun runs.
